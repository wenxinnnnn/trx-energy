Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Thank you for using TRX Energy Rental (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information generated when you use our service.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

This Platform is designed as a fully anonymous service. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • Account credentials (no registration required)
  • Your private keys or seed phrases (we never ask for them)
  • IP addresses (not actively logged)

2. Public Blockchain Data

All transactions on the TRON blockchain — including TRX transfers to our payment address — are publicly visible on-chain and can be queried by anyone via a block explorer. This is an inherent property of blockchain technology and is outside our control.

3. Telegram Support

If you contact us via Telegram, messages you send are processed by Telegram's platform. Please refer to Telegram's Privacy Policy. We will not use your Telegram information for marketing or share it with third parties.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

This Platform is a static website. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics tools.

5. Minors

This service is intended for adults with full legal capacity. It is not directed at persons under 18 years of age.

6. Policy Changes

If this Privacy Policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. We recommend checking periodically.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us via Telegram Support.
